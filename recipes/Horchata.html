Horchata

Home

Description

Horchata is classic, refreshing, and a true masterpiece of culinary and hispanic art. I love horchata it is a wonderful rice based drink that is sure to make good days great and uplift you from a bad day. Follow the recipe below for a step by step breakdown for making this delicious drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked long grain white rice
  • 1/2 cup blanched silver almonds
  • 1 Mexican cinnamon stick broken in half
  • 6 cups of water divided
  • 3 whole cups milk
  • 12 ounces evaporated milk
  • 1/2 - 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Ground cinnamon for topping

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cool water until the water runs clear.
  2. Soak the rinsed rice, almonds, cinnamon stick, and 3 cups of water in a covered bowl in the refrigerator for 8 hours, or up to 24 hours.
  3. Blend the soaked rice mixture along with 3 additional cups of water for several minutes until it is completely smooth. Based on the size of your blender, you may have to work in batches.
  4. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh strainer or a nut milk bag to get rid of the grittiness. You will need to strain the mixture 2-4 times to get a creamy and smooth consistency. It’s a bit of a tedious process but necessary to get it silky smooth.
  5. Transfer the rice mixture into a large pitcher and add the whole milk, evaporated milk, sugar, and vanilla. Stir together until the ingredients are fully combined.
  6. Serve over ice and garnish with ground cinnamon.
